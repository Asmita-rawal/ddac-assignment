Understanding Alzheimer's

Alzheimer's disease is a progressive brain disorder that affects memory, thinking, and behavior. One of the most concerning behaviors is wandering, which can put individuals at risk.

🧠

What is Alzheimer's?

Alzheimer's is the most common cause of dementia, accounting for 60-80% of cases. It causes problems with memory, thinking, and behavior that interfere with daily life.

🚶

Wandering Risk

About 60% of people with Alzheimer's will wander at some point. Wandering can occur even in the early stages and can happen at any time, day or night.

💚

How You Can Help

Stay alert, report sightings immediately, and learn how to approach a lost person with Alzheimer's. Your actions can save a life and reunite families.
