Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about CarePath

How do I report a missing person?

If you are a caregiver, log in to your account and go to the "Create Missing Report" section. If you are a community member, contact the authorities immediately and then use our "Submit Sighting" feature.

Is CarePath free to use?

Yes! CarePath is completely free for caregivers, community members, and administrators. We believe in supporting our community without any cost.

How do I become a caregiver?

Simply register for an account and select "Caregiver" as your role during registration. You can then start adding elderly profiles and managing missing reports.

What should I do if I see a missing person?

Stay with the person if safe to do so, call 911 immediately, and then use our "Submit Sighting" feature to alert the caregiver and authorities.
